I currently live in NYC but was born and raised in the wild untamed forests of Maine. I am a first generation "city dweller" and view buildings as trees and the people that work and live in them as elves that smell and dress better.
My passion: I LOVE DESIGN - I am always looking for new design inspiration. I find the best way to be inspired is to live for extended periods of time in and out of your comfort zones. Overstay your welcome and plan to never get a return invitation.
A fun fact: When I was a tween, while foraging for blueberries and pine cones one day, I discovered an old computer in a bush. Not knowing what it was at the time, I disassembled it then reconstructed it into a roof for my pop up eco shelter.
